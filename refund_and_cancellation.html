Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: 26-02-2026
App Name: Fotocolab

1. Subscription Model

Fotocolab offers optional premium subscriptions that provide access to additional features such as premium feeds, high-quality downloads, no watermark, unlimited downloads, hide ads, font accessibility, logo updates, signature updates, and address features.

Subscriptions are auto-renewing and will automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle unless cancelled before the renewal date.

2. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through Google Play In-App Purchases. Fotocolab does not collect or store your credit card, debit card, or banking details. All payment transactions are handled by Google in accordance with their payment policies and security standards.

3. Refund Policy

Refund requests are handled in accordance with Google Play’s refund policies. Fotocolab does not have the authority to approve, deny, or process refunds for purchases made through Google Play.

If you wish to request a refund, you must submit your request directly through the Google Play Store using the Google account used for the purchase.

4. Cancellation Policy

Users may cancel their subscription at any time through their Google Play account settings.

If a subscription is cancelled, premium access will remain active until the end of the current billing period. After that period, the subscription will not renew, and access to premium features will be discontinued.

5. Account Deletion

Deleting your Fotocolab account does not automatically cancel your subscription. Users must cancel their subscription directly through Google Play before deleting their account.

6. Technical Issues

In case of verified technical errors caused directly by the application that prevent access to purchased premium features, users may contact our support team for assistance.

7. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update or modify this Refund & Cancellation Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
