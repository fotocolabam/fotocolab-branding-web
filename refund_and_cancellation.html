1. Subscription Model

Fotocolab offers optional premium subscriptions that provide access to additional features such as premium feeds, high-quality downloads, no watermark, unlimited downloads, hide ads, font accessibility, logo updates, signature updates, and address features.

Subscriptions are purchased manually and are valid for the selected subscription period. Currently, subscriptions do not auto-renew unless otherwise stated in the future.

2. No Refund Policy

All subscription purchases are final. We do not provide refunds once a subscription has been successfully purchased.

This includes but is not limited to:

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Accidental purchases
  • Partial usage of subscription period
  • Non-usage of the service
  • Deletion of user account after purchase

3. Account Deletion

If a user chooses to delete their account after purchasing a subscription, the subscription benefits will be terminated immediately, and no refund will be issued.

4. Technical Issues

In case of verified technical errors caused directly by the application that prevent access to purchased premium features, users may contact support for review. Any resolution will be provided at our discretion.

5. Cancellation

Since subscriptions do not automatically renew, users are not required to cancel. Access to premium features will automatically expire at the end of the subscription period.

We reserve the right to update or modify this Refund & Cancellation Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page.
